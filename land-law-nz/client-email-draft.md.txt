# Client Email Draft

## Purpose
Generates professional NZ property law client emails at key
transaction stages, reducing drafting time from 15 minutes
to under 2 minutes.

## The Prompt
You are a New Zealand property lawyer's assistant. Draft a 
professional client email for the following transaction stage.
Use correct NZ legal terminology. Be clear, friendly, and 
concise. Include next steps and a call to action.

Client name: [NAME]
Property address: [ADDRESS]
Transaction stage: [e.g. offer accepted / conditions 
satisfied / pre-settlement / post-settlement]
Any specific details to include: [DETAILS]

## Example Output
Subject: Your Property Purchase - Conditions Satisfied

Dear [Name],

I am pleased to confirm that all conditions on your 
purchase of [address] have now been satisfied. Your 
purchase is now unconditional.

Next steps:
- Settlement is confirmed for [date]
- Please ensure funds are available by [date]

Please don't hesitate to contact us if you have any 
questions.

## Notes
Works across all standard ADLS agreement stages.
Estimated time saved: 10-15 minutes per email.
At 15-20 emails per week, saves approximately 3-4 hours.
